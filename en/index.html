Quick Tips

Practical advice from our Facebook Reels

01

Coffee grounds

Use coffee grounds as a natural fertilizer. Rich in nitrogen, they are perfect for hydrangeas, roses and tomatoes.

02

Water at the right time

Water early in the morning or late afternoon to avoid evaporation and leaf burn.

03

Eggshells

Crush eggshells around your plants to deter slugs and add calcium to the soil.

04

Companion planting

Plant basil near tomatoes: it repels whiteflies and improves the flavor of the fruit.

05

Cooking water

Save the cooking water from vegetables (cooled) to water your plants -- it's a nutrient-rich fertilizer.

06

Natural mulching

Mulch the base of your plants with dead leaves or straw to retain moisture and limit weeds.
