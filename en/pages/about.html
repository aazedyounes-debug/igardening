About Us

iGardening is a community of passionate gardeners. Our mission: to make gardening accessible to everyone, from beginners to experienced gardeners.

Our Story

iGardening was born from a simple passion: the joy of watching what you've planted grow. What started as a small gardening tips blog has become a growing community of gardeners who share their experiences, successes, and sometimes their failures.

We believe that everyone can have a green thumb. Whether you have a large garden, a small balcony, or even just a windowsill, there is always a way to grow something beautiful and delicious.

Our content is designed to be practical, accessible, and based on real experience. No unnecessary jargon, just tips that really work.
