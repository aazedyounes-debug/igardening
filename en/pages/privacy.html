Privacy Policy

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. Here is how we use and protect it.

1. Data Controller

The website iGardening.co is published by iGardening. For any question regarding the protection of your data, you can contact us at: contact@igardening.co.

2. Data Collected

We may collect the following data as part of using our website:

  • Browsing data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, visit duration, via analytical cookies.
  • Contact data: name, email address and message when you use our contact form.
  • Subscription data: email address when you sign up for our newsletter.

3. Purpose of Processing

Your data is used to:

  • Respond to your requests via the contact form.
  • Send you our newsletter if you have subscribed.
  • Improve our website and content through browsing statistics.
  • Ensure the proper technical functioning of the website.

4. Legal Basis

The processing of your data is based on:

  • Your consent for newsletter subscription and the use of non-essential cookies.
  • Our legitimate interest for traffic statistics and website improvement.
  • Performance of a contract for responding to your contact requests.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for:

  • Essential cookies: necessary for the website to function (no consent required).
  • Analytical cookies: to measure traffic and improve our content (with your consent).

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings.

6. Data Retention Period

  • Contact data: retained for 12 months after the last exchange.
  • Newsletter data: retained until you unsubscribe.
  • Browsing data: retained for a maximum of 13 months.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your data may only be shared with:

  • Our hosting provider (Vercel) for the technical operation of the website.
  • Our analytics service (if applicable) for anonymized statistics.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: obtain a copy of your personal data.
  • Right of rectification: correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: request the deletion of your data.
  • Right to portability: receive your data in a structured format.
  • Right to object: object to the processing of your data.
  • Right to withdraw consent: withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: contact@igardening.co.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, modification, disclosure or destruction. Our website uses the HTTPS protocol to secure data exchanges.

10. External Links

Our website contains links to third-party sites (Facebook, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We encourage you to read their respective privacy policies.

Last updated: March 11, 2026. We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be published on this page with the update date.
